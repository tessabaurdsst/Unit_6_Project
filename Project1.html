The Hardiest Flora on the Planet

What makes succulents so hardy? It's all in the leaves. The leaves are full of moisture and nutrients that help them thrive in dry and warm climates. You only need to water them once a week, which makes them super low maintenance. Whether you live in a mansion or a shack, succulents are a great plants to have!

Recently, succulents have become really popular. They go perfectly with the minimalist aesthetic, and don't require large amounts of space to maintain. You don't need a green thumb to keep them alive. Anyone can do it!
